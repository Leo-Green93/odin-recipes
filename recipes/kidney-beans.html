Red Kidney Beans and Rice

Description

This dish features red kidney beans stewed in a flavorful tomato sauce served over rice and is a healthy, flavorful addition to any dinner menu. Seriously, who knew simple could be so delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) red kidney beans
  • 6 oz ground meat or vegetarian substitute
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced or crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 large white or yellow onion, diced
  • 1 tsp fresh minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp dried cilantro
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tsp Adobo seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Add the olive oil to a pan and saut√© the onions and garlic over medium heat until soft and semi-transparent.
  2. Brown the ground meat (or vegetarian substitute), then add the broth, tomatoes, and seasonings to the pan.
  3. After the liquid has reduced to a sauce-like consistency, drain the kidney beans and gently stir them in to the mixture. Cook just long enough to warm, then remove from heat.
  4. Serve over fresh white or brown rice with sliced avocado, hot sauce, or any other toppings.
