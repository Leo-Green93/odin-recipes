Tofu Scramble

Description

Tofu scramble is a healthy and flavorful protein-packed meal that presents tofu alongside kale and tomatoes to jump-start your day.

This delicous dairy-free meal will win over even the firmest tofu critics!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz firm tofu, drained
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 diced tomato
  • 1 diced bell pepper
  • 1 cup chopped kale
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 cup oat milk (or other nondairy milk)
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Grease a nonstick pan, or add a little oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Saut√© the vegetables and garlic over medium heat until lightly browned.
  3. Drain the tofu, then add it to the pan. If using firm tofu, break it up with a spatula (silken tofu will break up on its own as you stir).
  4. Combine the oat milk, nutritional yeast, curry powder, onion powder, turmeric, and salt in a small bowl. Pour over the browned tofu.
  5. Stir in the tomatoes and kale. Cook just until the kale is bright green and slightly wilted.
  6. Serve with toast, home fries, sliced avocado, salsa or hot sauce,
